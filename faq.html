Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about MoCap Free Beta.

Is MoCap Free free?

Yes. MoCap Free Beta is completely free.

Which browsers are supported?

Google Chrome Microsoft Edge Safari

What file formats are supported?

JSON BVH GLB

Can I use it with Godot?

Yes. MoCap Free is designed primarily for Godot workflows.

Can I use it with Blender?

Yes. BVH and GLB can be imported into Blender.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Everything runs directly in your browser.

Will more export formats be added?

Yes. VRM FBX and additional export options are planned.

Is commercial use allowed?

The Beta version is under development. Please check the Terms of Service before commercial use.
