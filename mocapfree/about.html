About MoCap Free

A free browser-based motion capture tool.

Our Mission

MoCap Free was created to make motion capture accessible to everyone. Instead of requiring expensive hardware or complicated software, MoCap Free runs directly in your browser.

Current Features

  • ✔ Browser Based
  • ✔ Video Upload
  • ✔ JSON Export
  • ✔ BVH Export
  • ✔ GLB Export
  • ✔ Godot Ready

Roadmap

  • 🟡 VRM Preview
  • 🟡 FBX Export
  • 🟡 Motion Cleanup
  • 🟡 Finger Tracking
  • 🟡 AI Motion Retarget
  • 🟡 Multi-language Support

Why MoCap Free?

Many motion capture solutions require paid software or complex workflows. MoCap Free aims to provide a simple workflow:

Upload → Capture → Download

Beta Version

MoCap Free is currently in Beta. Some features are still under development and may change in future releases. Feedback is always welcome.
