User Guide

Everything you need to start using MoCap Free.

1. Upload a Video

Click Start Capture and select a video file. Supported formats:

  • MP4
  • MOV
  • WEBM

2. Capture Motion

MoCap Free analyzes your video inside the browser. No installation required.

3. Export

Download your motion as

  • JSON
  • BVH
  • GLB

4. Preview

Open GLB Viewer and inspect your generated animation.

5. Import to Godot

Import the exported GLB or BVH into your Godot project.
