Privacy Policy

How MoCap Free handles your information.

1. Overview

MoCap Free respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected while using this website.

2. Uploaded Files

Videos uploaded to MoCap Free are processed for motion capture generation. During the Beta phase, processing is designed to occur locally in your browser whenever possible.

We do not intentionally store uploaded videos on our servers unless a future feature explicitly requires cloud processing.

3. Cookies

MoCap Free may use cookies to improve user experience and remember preferences.

4. Analytics

Anonymous analytics may be used to understand how the website is used and improve future versions.

5. Advertising

Future versions of MoCap Free may display advertisements. Advertising partners may use cookies according to their own privacy policies.

6. Third-party Services

The website may include links to external services such as GitHub or other developer resources. Those services have their own privacy policies.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the service evolves. Please check this page periodically.

8. Contact

Contact information will be added before the official release.
